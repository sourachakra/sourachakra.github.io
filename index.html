Hello World! I am a Research Assistant in the Computer Vision (CV) lab at Stony Brook University under the supervision of Prof. Dimitris Samaras.

Research Interest: My research interests lie in the areas of computer vision and machine learning. In the recent past, my focus has been on studying and predicting human visual attention. Specifically, I have developed deep learning models that can predict human visual attention in natural images, webpages, graphic designs, whole slide images of cancer tissues, etc. I have also worked in the domains of generative AI and self-supervised segmentation.

Coursework: Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Data Visualization, Computer Graphics

Prior to joining Stony Brook University, I completed my Masters degree from UC Santa Barbara, USA and IIT Kharagpur, India in the domain of signal processing. I completed by undergraduate degree from NIT Durgapur, India in Electronics and Communication Engineering.
